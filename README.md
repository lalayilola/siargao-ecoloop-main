## 🔄 The Circular Cycle

```
Farmer Lists Produce
    ↓
Restaurant Purchases
    ↓
Transaction Completed (Points awarded)
    ↓
Restaurant Uses Products
    ↓
Restaurant Reports Waste
    ↓
LGU Collects & Processes
    ↓
LGU Creates Compost
    ↓
Farmer Purchases Compost
    ↓
Farmer Uses in Crops
    ↓
CYCLE CONTINUES...
```

Every step is tracked, real-time, and generates eco points.
